The Game Day Grill is a locally owned grill in the small town of New Plymouth, ID. We are all about satisfying your game day needs whether it's your hunger or your entertainment.The grill consists of four large flat screen TVs, always playing what you want. We started this business because we wanted to enjoy our time with locals and more. We wanted to meet more people and give the satisfication our community deserves.
